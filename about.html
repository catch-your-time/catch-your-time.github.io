About

Time is fleetly. Sometimes we cannot remember what we were doing yesterday. It escapes from us.

Time is a limited resource. We often forget it. We should not spend it in vain, so that it does not hurt for the aimlessly spent years.

We often go by a circle without noticing it. Our memory is not able to remember all, but technologies are able to help us.

The Catch your time application is a calendar organized in a certain way where you may write a target which should be completed in a week. A square is a week. A row is an year. A current week is highlighted. Grey squares are past, blue ones are future. Your calendar starts from your birth year to one plus life expectancy in your country for men or women.

To create a target you need to login on a Google account. You have to allow this app to get data about your birthday, gender and an ability to create a Google calendar. Your targets are stored in secure Google repositories. The app does not use any Google calendars you have already created!

After that, you will be able to create your targets and see them on a scale of your life. The app will remind you that you should create a target per week. Catch your time!
